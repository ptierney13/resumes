## Posting Text

Platform Engineers own the full lifecycle of infrastructure features-from design and implementation through production monitoring and support with a touch of security. You'll build and maintain the systems that empower product teams to ship faster, working in an async, high-ownership culture. We love folks who love code, solving cool problems, and materializing great ideas.

### What you'll be doing

- Design and maintain scalable, reproducible infrastructure using Terraform, with a strong emphasis on security and automation across environments
- Build and support backend services in TypeScript, NestJS, and Node.js, with MongoDB and Postgres as primary data stores
- Own and optimize AWS infrastructure across EC2, ECS, RDS, S3, and CloudWatch
- Develop reusable infrastructure modules and internal tooling that reduce friction for product engineering teams
- Take features from design through deployment and ongoing production support
- Collaborate asynchronously across a globally distributed remote team

### Qualifications

- Experience in high-growth startup or similarly fast-paced environments
- Hands-on background with cloud infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, and production systems with a strong DevOps mindset
- Comfortable building and supporting backend systems using modern cloud-native frameworks
- Strong interest in infrastructure as code, automation, and platform-level improvements
- Clear, proactive communicator who can work cross-functionally and surface problems early
