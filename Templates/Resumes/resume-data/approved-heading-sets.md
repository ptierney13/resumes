# Approved Heading Sets

These are previously accepted subsection structures for organizing the Microsoft section or equivalent top-level experience blocks.

## `devops-infra-default`

### Headings

- `CI/CD & Internal Tooling`
- `Observability & Telemetry`
- `Reliability & Incident Response`
- `API & Data Layer Development`
- `Platform & Framework Development`

### Use When

- The role is clearly DevOps, SRE, reliability, observability, QA infrastructure, or infra-leaning backend.
- You want a clean split between operational categories so CI/CD, observability, and reliability each get their own lane.

### Highlights

- CI/CD ownership
- Telemetry and observability
- Incident response and production reliability
- Supporting backend/API work

### Lacks

- A strong "shared systems other engineers depend on" platform center of gravity
- A compact platform story when the role is broader than DevOps

## `platform-systems-default`

### Headings

- `Platform & Framework Development`
- `Operations, Reliability, & Production Support`
- `Backend Services & APIs`

### Use When

- The role is platform engineering, backend platform, developer infrastructure, or shared-systems oriented.
- You want the resume to read first as reusable platform/system ownership and only second as operations support.

### Highlights

- Shared abstractions and internal platforms
- Systems other engineers depend on
- Full lifecycle ownership from implementation through production support

### Lacks

- Fine-grained separation between CI/CD, telemetry, and reliability themes
- A pure DevOps/SRE framing when the role is explicitly operations-first

## `team-chronology`

### Headings

- `AI Hub Team`
- `Whiteboard Team`
- `Edge Telemetry Team`
- `Whiteboard Team` / `Layout Engine Team` under Software Engineer I

### Use When

- Team chronology and direct organizational placement matter more than thematic grouping.
- A narrative by team or project history reads more naturally than a class-of-work structure.

### Highlights

- Chronological clarity
- Concrete team placement
- Easier mapping to specific product groups

### Lacks

- Strong role-class framing for DevOps or platform-specific job families
- Fast keyword scanning for a specific operational or platform theme
