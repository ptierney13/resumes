# Assurance DevOps Kickoff

## Use When

- The role emphasizes DevOps, release tooling, artifact/package promotion, compliance or assurance guardrails, or regulated-environment operational controls.
- The strongest story should center on CI/CD ownership, telemetry guardrails, package publishing and promotion, and production-safe operational workflows.

## What This Resume Highlights

- CI/CD and testing systems
- Artifact/package publishing, promotion, and rollback workflows
- Telemetry guardrails and regulated-environment logging controls
- Reliability, incident response, and operational runbooks
- Supporting backend/auth and Azure infrastructure work

## What It De-Emphasizes

- Broader platform-only framing when the role is more release/assurance-oriented
- Generic backend/API emphasis that is not central to the role
- Unsupported security-specialist claims such as SBOM/SLSA depth, OCI expertise, or deep audit ownership
