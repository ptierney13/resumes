# DevOps / Infra Kickoff

## Use When

- The role emphasizes DevOps, SRE, operational excellence, CI/CD, observability, release quality, or incident response.
- The strongest story should center on production support, telemetry, testing systems, and internal operational tooling.

## What This Resume Highlights

- CI/CD and testing systems
- Observability and telemetry
- Reliability and incident response
- Azure-centered infrastructure support
- Operational runbooks and support models

## What It De-Emphasizes

- Platform migration and framework ownership as the lead story
- Backend/API accomplishments that are better as supporting evidence than the center of gravity
- Broader platform framing that is more natural in the platform kickoff
