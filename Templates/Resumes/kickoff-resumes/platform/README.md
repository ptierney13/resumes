# Platform Kickoff

## Use When

- The role emphasizes platform engineering, internal systems, backend platforms, shared tooling, or systems that unblock other engineers.
- The strongest story should center on reusable systems, shared abstractions, and platform ownership from implementation through production support.

## What This Resume Highlights

- Shared platforms and systems other engineers depend on
- Internal tooling and developer enablement
- Platform migration and framework ownership
- Operational ownership and production support
- Backend and security-supporting services

## What It De-Emphasizes

- Fine-grained separation between CI/CD, telemetry, and reliability categories
- Team chronology as the main organizing principle
- Purely DevOps/SRE framing when the role is broader than operations
